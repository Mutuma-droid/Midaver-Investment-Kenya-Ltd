About Us

Midaver Investment: Where need meets Solution.
At Midaver Investment, we provide reliable and timely loan services tailored to meet your financial needs. Our commitment is to support individuals and businesses with quick and convenient access to credit.
We offer three distinct loan products designed to suit different needs and repayment preferences:
  • Boresha Biashara Loan- Empowering your business growth.
  • Quick Weekly Loan- Fast, short-term financial support.
  • Monthly Quick Loan- Convenient monthly repayment options.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and small businesses by providing fast, reliable, and accessible loan solutions that support financial stability and growth.

Our Vision
